Our Visionaries

The driving force behind the Institute's mission to uplift and empower the community.

Wilfred Peter “Sedi” Elrington

Founder & Patron

Wilfred Elrington involves a distinguished career as a Belizean politician and lawyer. Serving as the Foreign Minister of Belize since 2008 and representing the Pickstock constituency, he has dedicated his life to public service.


An alumnus of the University of the West Indies, he has held key executive, legislative, and judicial roles. In 2009, he founded the Samuel Haynes Institute of Excellence to directly address community needs, focusing on education for disadvantaged youth. His personal interests include farming, journalism, and tireless community service. He is married to Barbara Elrington, with whom he shares four children.

Barbara Elrington

Senior Consultant & Community Leader

A pillar of strength and guidance for the Institute, Barbara Elrington serves as Senior Consultant. She is deeply involved in guiding the Institute’s programs and outreach initiatives, ensuring they remain relevant and impactful.


Barbara is a strong advocate for youth development and has served as an executive director for various community and charitable initiatives. Frequently seen alongside Wilfred Elrington at institute events, she continues to champion the cause of community transformation and empowerment.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

The Samuel Haynes Institute of Excellence ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you visit our website.

2. Information Collection

This website is informational. We do not collect personal data via cookies or tracking pixels. Any information you provide via email or contact forms (if applicable) is used solely to respond to your inquiry.

3. Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies. We may use essential local storage to remember your preference for the mobile menu state, but this data never leaves your device.

4. Third-Party Services

We embed videos from YouTube using their "Privacy-Enhanced Mode" (youtube-nocookie.com). This means YouTube will not store information about you unless you play the video.

5. Children's Privacy

As a youth-focused organization, we comply with COPPA. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 online.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us at info@samuelhaynes.org.
